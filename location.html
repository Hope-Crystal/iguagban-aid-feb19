vision and mission

To create and foster a sense of cooperation,integration and unity, also to establish self-substainable solutions within the community.


Sons and daughters of Iguagban community, in abroad have come together to inspire and empower our disadvantaged members of our community back home. This is to establish, motivate and foster a sense of togetherness and unity amongst us all, regardless of our residence. We have drafted self- sustainable solutions for all people living in the community, old and young, in order to guarantee the development for this and the next generation and also beyond, in accordance with the global trend.
