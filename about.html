our history

We are a group of people living in abroad but born and raised in Iguagban. Our parents are/were farmers and live/d in this village. Our aim is to give back to our community, establish necessary social amenities in our village, for example roads, clean water, education, healthcare and electricity. The association was established on 23th December 2020. We currently have 56 members

vision and mission

To create and foster a sense of cooperation,integration and unity, also to establish self-substainable solutions within the community.

History of Iguagban

Today the main income comes form farming. The people belong to the Bini tribe of Edo State. The Community leader is Mr Aigbede, he acts as The Mediator and gives recommendations to the community. Also, he leads the community meetings in Nigeria.

Newly appointed is Mr Ekhosuehi Lugard as Chairman for the NGO In Nigeria.
He will assist to achieve improvements in the community.
Iguagban is a very welcoming community and accepts anyone into their midst.
