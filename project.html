Our Projects

Check out our work and case studies.

Clean Water (Borehole)

How We Did It

Dirty water can cause serious diseases, so our first accomplished goal was to drill a borehole and install a water pump with a general water well. Now our community has 365 days access to clean water.

Providing Good Roads

How We Did It

We have accomplished a new road to the community, therefore access to the city, which is essential for the farmers, so that they can offer their products to the broader market in the city and self-sustain. Now our aim is to keep it in order with yearly maintenance.

Education

How We Did It

Our community-based education project seeks to tackle the problem of child illiteracy which is common in rural Nigeria. We are engaging in building a new school because the old one is beyond repair. Our idea is to provide a modern facility to better the education of our youths. Also, we are setting up a scholarship/sponsorship program for our students, whose legal guardian can not afford the financial burden. Furthermore, we need all hands-on deck to achieve these goals. Your donations will go a long way to impact the quality and improve the life of all in the long run.

Health Care

How We Did It

General wellbeing is key to any human being; therefore, we want to establish a community health care centre. This will help all members in the community to access primary essential healthcare, from regular check-ups, vaccinations, antenatal checks and preventing, treatment and monitoring of chronic conditions for example blood pressure monitoring and blood sugar control.

Electricity

How We Did It

Solar energy is our future project for sustainable energy solution and security, which will give our community energy independence and also an environmentally friendly solution. This will simplify their everyday life and give them opportunity to improve educational resources as then simple computer literacy can be learned.
